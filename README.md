# Quranify-Privacy-Policy
Privacy Policy — Quranify

Last updated: 18 June 2026

Quranify ("the extension") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the extension handles.

DATA WE COLLECT
We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

DATA STORED ON YOUR DEVICE
Your preferences — selected reciter, surah, interface language, translation language, theme, and playback settings — are saved locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the extension.

NETWORK REQUESTS
To play recitations and show translations, the extension requests audio files and verse text from the public Quran.com API (api.quran.com and quranicaudio.com). These requests are necessary for the extension to function. We do not control these third-party services; please refer to Quran.com's own policies regarding their API.

CHANGES
Any changes to this policy will be reflected on this page.
